🧠 What is AI? 🤔

What in the World is Artificial Intelligence?

Imagine if your toys could think and learn, just like you! That's kind of what Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is all about. It's like giving a computer a "brain" so it can solve problems, play games, and even be creative!

AI is not about robots taking over the world like in the movies. It's about teaching computers to be helpful assistants. Think about a smart speaker that plays your favorite song when you ask, or a game that knows how to play against you. That's AI in action!
